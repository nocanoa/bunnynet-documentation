Storage pricing is based on your storage tier and the number of replication regions you enable. View current pricing at [bunny.net/pricing](https://bunny.net/pricing).

## Standard tier (HDD)

**Pricing structure:**

- **Single region**: \$0.01/GB
- **Two regions**: \$0.02/GB
- **Three regions**: \$0.025/GB
- **Each additional region**: +\$0.005/GB
- **Maximum**: Up to 9 regions

The first two regions cost \$0.01/GB each. Additional regions beyond two cost \$0.005/GB each.

**Example calculations:**

- 1 region: \$0.01/GB
- 2 regions: \$0.02/GB (2 × \$0.01)
- 3 regions: \$0.025/GB (2 × \$0.01 + 1 × \$0.005)
- 4 regions: \$0.03/GB (2 × \$0.01 + 2 × \$0.005)
- 9 regions: \$0.055/GB (2 × \$0.01 + 7 × \$0.005)

## Edge tier (SSD)

**Pricing structure:**

- **Single region**: \$0.02/GB
- **Each additional region**: +\$0.02/GB
- **Maximum**: Up to 15 regions

Each region costs \$0.02/GB, including the primary region.

**Example calculations:**

- 1 region: \$0.02/GB
- 2 regions: \$0.04/GB (2 × \$0.02)
- 3 regions: \$0.06/GB (3 × \$0.02)
- 15 regions: \$0.30/GB (15 × \$0.02)

## What's included

Both storage tiers include:

- True pay-as-you-go pricing
- \$1 monthly minimum
- **FREE traffic to Bunny CDN**
- **No API fees**
- **Free API egress**
- RAID 6 storage configuration
- Eleven nines durability (with multiple replication regions)
- Automatic failover and recovery

## Bandwidth costs

Traffic served via Bunny CDN is billed separately according to CDN pricing. However:

- Traffic from Storage to CDN is **FREE**
- API egress is **FREE**
- Only end-user CDN delivery is billed
