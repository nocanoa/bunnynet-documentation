---
title: Close Account
description: How to close your bunny.net account.
---

If you'd like to stop using bunny.net, you can close your account from the [Account Settings](https://dash.bunny.net/account/settings) page, or go directly to the [Close Account](https://dash.bunny.net/account/settings/close) page.

<Note>
  Thinking about leaving? We'd love to hear from you first. We actively listen
  to feedback and build what our users need to scale. If something's missing for
  you, there's a good chance we're already working on it. [Get in
  touch](https://bunny.net/contact) before you go.
</Note>

## Before closing your account

<Warning>
  Closing your account is permanent and cannot be reversed. All your data will
  be deleted and your account balance cannot be refunded.
</Warning>

Before proceeding:

- Delete all your Pull Zones and Storage Zones
- Download any files you want to keep
- Export any data or configurations you may need

## Close your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Close Account">
    Navigate to [Account Settings](https://dash.bunny.net/account/settings) and
    click **Close My Account**, or go directly to the [Close
    Account](https://dash.bunny.net/account/settings/close) page.
    <Frame>
      ![Close account section](/images/accounts-and-billing/close-account.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a reason">
    Select a reason for closing your account. This feedback helps us improve the
    service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm closure">
    Click **Close My Account Forever** to complete the account closure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
