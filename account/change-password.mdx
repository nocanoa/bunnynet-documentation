---
title: Change Password
description: Update your bunny.net account password.
---

You can change your password at any time from the Account Settings page.

## Change your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Change Password">
    Navigate to [Account Settings](https://dash.bunny.net/account/settings) and
    click **Change Password**, or go directly to the [Change
    Password](https://dash.bunny.net/account/settings/changepassword) page.
    
    <Frame>
      ![Change password](/images/accounts-and-billing/change-password.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new password">
    Enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password.
    
    Your password must contain:

    - At least 12 characters
    - One special character
    - One lowercase letter
    - One uppercase letter
    - One number
    </Step>

  <Step title="Save">Click **Save** to update your password.</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  For additional account security, consider enabling [two-factor
  authentication](/accounts-and-billing/two-factor-authentication).
</Note>

## Forgot your password?

If you've forgotten your password and can't log in, use the **Forgot Password** link on the [login page](https://dash.bunny.net) to reset it via email.
