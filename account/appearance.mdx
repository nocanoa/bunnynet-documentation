You can customize the appearance of the bunny.net dashboard by choosing between light and dark mode.

## Change your theme

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    Navigate to [Account Settings](https://dash.bunny.net/account/settings) in
    your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Appearance section">
    Scroll to the **Appearance** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your theme">
    Click the light or dark mode icon to switch themes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>![Theme selection](/images/accounts-and-billing/appearance.png)</Frame>

Your theme preference is saved automatically and applies across all your dashboard sessions.
