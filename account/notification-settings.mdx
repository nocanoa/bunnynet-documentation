Manage your email preferences from the [Account Settings](https://dash.bunny.net/account/settings) page.

<Frame>
  ![Notification settings
  toggles](/images/accounts-and-billing/notification-settings.png)
</Frame>

## Notification emails

Emails about your account balance and other account-related notifications.

<Note>
  We strongly recommend leaving this enabled in case we need to alert you about
  something.
</Note>

## Promotional emails

If enabled, we might send you an offer or service update news from time to time. You can disable these at any time without affecting your account notifications.

## Changing notification preferences

Toggle each email type on or off using the switches in the Notification settings section. Changes save automatically.
