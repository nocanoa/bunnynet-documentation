The [Data Processing Agreement](https://dash.bunny.net/account/dpa) page lets you view, accept, and download the GDPR data processing agreement for your account.

## Accepting the agreement

The agreement is pre-filled with your account details. Review the terms and click **Accept** to sign the agreement.

## Downloading the agreement

Once accepted, you can download a copy of the signed agreement for your records.
