---
title: "Delete App"
description: "Permanently remove a container and its configuration."
---

<Warning>
  Deleting an app is irreversible. Ensure you want to permanently remove the app
  before confirming.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your container">
    Go to **Magic Containers** and select the container you want to delete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the menu">
    Click the menu icon (three dots) on the right side of the screen and click **Delete App**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/5d464940ef3caaee7f12b78d212efe576adac987f2c3e3467046bca9702a9f53-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Enter the name of the app to confirm, then click **Confirm**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/c8bd0c3705c48175cc39c11ca710a445c2ef32ec4722364de0657327b2b2c886-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>
