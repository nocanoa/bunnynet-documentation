---
title: "Delete App"
description: "Deleting an app in Magic Containers is a straightforward process. Follow the steps below to remove an app from your account:"
---

1. Login to [bunny.net dashboard](https://dash.bunny.net/auth/login?pk_buttonlocation=menu).

2. Navigate to the **Magic Containers** section and select the app you want to remove.

3. Click on the menu icon (three horizontal dots) on the right side of the screen and click **Delete App**.

<Frame>
![](/images/docs/5d464940ef3caaee7f12b78d212efe576adac987f2c3e3467046bca9702a9f53-image.png)
</Frame>

A popup message will appear, asking you to confirm the deletion.

4. In the popup, enter the name of the app you want to delete in the provided field and click the **Confirm** button.

<Frame>
![](/images/docs/c8bd0c3705c48175cc39c11ca710a445c2ef32ec4722364de0657327b2b2c886-image.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  ### Warning!

Deleting an app is irreversible. Ensure you want to permanently remove the app before confirming the action.

</Warning>

You can also remove your app by selecting the **Magic Containers** section, clicking on the **menu icon**, and selecting the option **delete**:

<Frame>
![](/images/docs/43eb3244fcf67585d4449d2d6a797d83df4648d0bfafd55a40252aa837f4e9f7-image.png)
</Frame>

Congratulations! You have successfully deleted your app in Magic Containers. If you have any concerns or encounter issues during the deletion process, please don't hesitate to contact us via the support request form for prompt assistance.
