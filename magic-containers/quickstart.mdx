This guide walks you through deploying your first application with Magic Containers.

<Info>
If you are using a trial account:

- You will need to add valid payment card details to unlock trial features.
- Trial accounts are limited to five regions, with up to two instances per region.

</Info>

## Before you begin

If you're deploying from a private registry, [connect your image registry](/magic-containers/image-registries) first. Public Docker Hub and GitHub images work without additional setup.

## Deploy your application

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new app">
    In the bunny.net dashboard, go to **Magic Containers** and click **Add App**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/7c23cfd96b446b8deab0096c5c9d2c8e07449b954653436cd35f93ab91112b23-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure deployment settings">
    Define the name of your app and select the deployment option (**Magic deployment**, **Single region deployment** or **Advanced deployment**), then click **Next Step**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/1a494e346f15d00d6bc158d19c64c70e5a07ec8dbd55d5df48a73b52cf9ce3b9-MC_Deploy_page.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a container">
    Click **Add Container**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/185a1e9579a90115450467c9bf2e76caf88ac5cd8e8083d07149b6810484824e-image.png)
    </Frame>

    Name your container (ensure each container has a unique name within the application).

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/d7aac2b139e515f46873646144a6b73e5dc39b6af69b2d6d959eb816e0f4e302-image.png)
    </Frame>

    Select the container registry, image (only `linux/amd64` images are supported), and tag.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure an endpoint">
    To make your app available on the internet, go to the **Endpoints** tab and click **Add New Endpoint**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/ff6201592ea4228a118dc628ac90e566fda918a7fcb6111f5858e7595c701de5-image.png)
    </Frame>

    Fill in the required information:

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/b123e4e93b2f164c151e61212f6051efa53523a2c26d7d2b399abb30a09ae709-image.png)
    </Frame>

    - **Name** - A unique name for the endpoint
    - **Expose via** - CDN or Anycast
    - **Container port** - The port your application listens on inside the container
    - **SSL for origin** - Whether your container uses SSL (CDN only)

    <Info>
    For more information on exposing your app to the internet, see the [endpoints guide](/magic-containers/endpoints).
    </Info>

    Click **Add endpoint**, then click **Add Container**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and deploy">
    Review your settings and click **Next Step**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/aab12e2eb0c3404e4b24f164b6f73cd2586760988b27ca14726125af5671bb2c-image.png)
    </Frame>

    Click **Confirm and Create**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/29094b8b92f25885168fd137ec1367f678b14b71a88d0ed18f18192abcf04800-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>

You will be navigated to your app's overview screen. The processing button should turn green, indicating that your app is being deployed.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/docs/088796ede6fd82ee1b4a899568fcb21b77a13c6f394f40ae0a5b1b5e4f1cedab-image.png)
</Frame>

Your application is now deployed and can be accessed by clicking the endpoint URL:

<Frame>
  ![](/images/docs/8ef79c614b1402619f36e3556b3eda7d76674394d20716e73fe2290f79fbde51-image.png)
</Frame>

Use the menu on the right side of the screen to restart, deploy, rename, or delete the app:

<Frame>
  ![](/images/docs/aca5804b3c52f1ce670cf53406257c143b9185b8faa1857da651b1bfd2cbb3cc-image.png)
</Frame>
