---
title: "Update App"
description: "Update your container to a new image version."
---

To update a [deployed container](/magic-containers/quickstart) to a new image version, make sure the new version is available in your container registry, then:

1. Go to **Magic Containers** and select the container you want to update.

2. Click **Container Settings**, then click **Edit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/docs/78520bc0c71f7abcae3b4b337cb5d555eaf8f1e05c56f1dafb1f46191747305e-image.png)
</Frame>

3. Select the new version from the image dropdown and click **Update Container**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/docs/b027eeb48ed941ddb96c1f4546b7180563962203c528bfe53638fe8728d53356-image.png)
</Frame>
