---
title: "Undeploy"
description: "Temporarily stop a running container without deleting it."
---

Undeploying stops your container but preserves its configuration, allowing you to redeploy later.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your container">
    Go to **Magic Containers** and select the container you want to undeploy.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the menu">
    Click the menu icon (three dots) on the right side of the screen and click **Undeploy**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/65f6d32b7064846ba892b90440e4d9d0ceed334c1fea8ace0f39a92cceb8c161-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Undeploy** to confirm.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/4fb6496c6704494ab7ed8ccebfc931cf57057c823b0e7b75421b2c0cd4abd06b-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>
