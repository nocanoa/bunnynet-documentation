bunny.net operates on a Pay As You Go model. You add credit to your account, and charges are deducted as you use services. You only pay for what you actually use.

You can add funds from the [Billing](https://dash.bunny.net/account/billing) page by clicking **Recharge Account**.

<Frame>![Add funds](/images/accounts-and-billing/recharge-account.png)</Frame>

## Adding funds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to [Billing](https://dash.bunny.net/account/billing) in your
    dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Recharge Account">
    Click the **Recharge Account** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the amount">
    Select an amount: \$10, \$25, \$50, \$100, \$250, \$500, \$1000, \$2000, or
    enter a custom amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a payment method">
    Choose **Card or PayPal** or **Crypto**, then select or add a payment
    method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Click **Pay** to add the funds to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover
- JCB
- Diner's Club

<Note>
  Your credit card information is never sent to bunny.net. Payment details are
  securely stored by Braintree.
</Note>

## How billing works

### Traffic billing

You are charged for each outbound byte transferred from the bunny.net network. Incoming traffic from your server to our network is free. If a 1GB video is viewed 100 times, you are charged for 100GB. Partial downloads are only charged for the bytes transferred.

### Storage billing

Storage is charged based on usage in the past period, usually less than 24 hours. Data that is uploaded and deleted within this period is only charged for that time, not the whole month.

### Minimum monthly usage

Your credits never expire. However, there is a \$1 minimum monthly usage while you have active zones on your account. If your usage is below \$1, it will be rounded up to $1 for that month. This applies to the entire account, not per zone.

### Minimum account balance

You need to maintain a positive balance to keep your services online. If your balance drops low, you'll receive warning emails. If your account is not recharged, your account will be suspended and your pull zones disabled. Data in storage zones will be deleted after a few days without backup.

<Note>
  To avoid interruptions, consider enabling
  [Auto-Recharge](/billing/auto-recharge) to automatically add funds when your
  balance gets low.
</Note>
