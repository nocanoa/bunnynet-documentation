The [Billing History](https://dash.bunny.net/account/billing-history) page shows all your account transactions, including monthly usage, payments, and applied promo codes.

<Frame>
  ![Billing history](/images/accounts-and-billing/billing-history.png)
</Frame>

## What you'll see

The billing history displays:

- **Date** of the transaction
- **Description** of the charge or credit
- **Amount**
- **Type** (Monthly usage, PayPal, Coupon code, etc.)

A chart at the top shows your account balance over time.

## Downloading invoices

For payments made via PayPal or card, click **Download invoice** to get a PDF invoice for your records.

## Downloading usage summaries

For monthly usage entries, click **Download summary** to get a detailed breakdown of your charges for that period.

The usage summary includes:

- Your billing details
- Starting and ending balance
- Total activity, credits, payments, and refunds
- Itemized usage by service (traffic by region, storage, scripting, etc.)

<Note>
  Usage summaries are not invoices. They are a detailed breakdown of billing
  activity for the stated period.
</Note>
