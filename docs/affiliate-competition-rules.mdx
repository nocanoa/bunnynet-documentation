### Let’s hop into it!

Hey there, affiliate adventurer!

Want to win prizes by sharing Bunny with your audience? Just post your affiliate link on socials, blogs, videos (anywhere you connect with your followers), and bring in real users.

We’re counting **active bunnies:** people who start actually using bunny.net by pulling traffic, storing files, streaming, or building.

Before you start spreading the Bunny magic, here’s everything you need to know.

### Timeline:

- **Starts:** September 1, 2025
- **Ends:** September 27, 2025 (midnight UTC)
- Winners announced the first week of October

### Who can join the competition?

Everyone. Seriously.

There’s **no extra signup needed.** If you’re a bunny.net affiliate, you’re **already in**!

However, please note that only the users you bring in **between Sept 1 and 27** will count. This way everyone has an equal start in the race.

### How to enter the Affiliate program?

1. **Log in** to your [bunny.net dashboard](https://bunny.net/).
2. Go to the **Affiliate** section: [https://dash.bunny.net/account/affiliate](https://dash.bunny.net/account/affiliate)
3. **Enable your affiliate link**.
4. Start sharing! Tweet it, blog it, post it, it’s your call.

### How to win

**Make someone’s internet experience better by introducing them to bunny.net!**

Share your affiliate link and bring in **active users,** people who actually use bunny.net to deliver content, store files, stream, or build. These are the users that count toward the competition, not just signups.

And yes, it’s worth it:

- **Top 3 affiliates** with the most active users win **Amazon gift cards** ($250 / $150 / $100)
- The **top 10 affiliates** get an **exclusive bunny.net merch box**
- And to keep it fun, **10 affiliates with at least 1 active user** will be randomly selected to win a **Bunny T-shirt & socks combo**

Even one active user could score you some bunny gear and help someone else enjoy a faster, fairer internet.

### A few quick rules

- Only the active users brought in via your **affiliate link** count.
- Users must **actually use** the platform, not just sign up and bounce.
- We’ll only count users referred **between September 1 and 27**.
- Please, no spammy or shady behavior. Play fair, we’re all about good vibes. (Want to know more about do’s and don’t? Check out our [Affiliate Guidelines](/docs/affiliate-guidelines)).
- This competition is open to all **registered bunny.net affiliates**.

### Need help?

- Peek into the [**Affiliate Best Practices Manual**](/docs/best-practices-manual) and turn your hustle into hoppin’ results!
- Or hop into our **Discord Affiliate channel!** Our team and fellow bunnies are happy to help.

Let the bunny race begin.

Good luck, and may the most active bunnies win!

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Do I need to sign up for the competition separately?**

Nope! All registered affiliates are automatically in. Just make sure your link is active and start sharing during the competition period, Sept 1–27.

---

**What counts as an “active user”?**

An active user is someone who doesn’t just sign up but actually starts using bunny.net: pulling traffic, storing files, or hopping into our services.

---

**I already brought in users before Sept 1. Do they count?**

We love your dedication, but only users referred **between Sept 1 and Sept 27** will count for the competition.

---

**Where do I find my affiliate link?**

Log in to your bunny.net dashboard, head to the Affiliate section, activate your link—and you’re all set! Or just click [here](https://dash.bunny.net/account/affiliate) to hop straight there.

---

**How will I know where I stand in the competition?**

You’ll see your live stats right on the affiliate dashboard during the competition.

There, you can track how many active users have been referred overall, and most importantly, **how many active users you’ve brought in since September 1st**.

It updates in real time, so you’ll always know exactly how you're doing.

---

**How will I get my prize if I win?**

We’ll reach out to winners directly after the competition ends to arrange delivery of gift cards, merch boxes, or swag!

---

**Where do I go for tips on promoting my link?**

Check out our [Affiliate Best Practices Manual](/docs/best-practices-manual) and join our **Discord** community for help, ideas, and bunny-powered motivation!

---

**Can I use ads or automated tools to promote my link?**

Only if it follows our [General Affiliate Guidelines](/docs/affiliate-guidelines). No shady stuff, please! Keep it clean, creative, and respectful.
